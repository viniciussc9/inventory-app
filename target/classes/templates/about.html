About Cozy Glow Candle Shop

Cozy Glow Candle Shop is a small business dedicated to creating hand-poured, eco-friendly candles in a variety of soothing scents. Every candle is made with care using high-quality wax, natural wicks, and premium fragrance oils.

Our mission is to bring warmth and comfort to your home through the soft glow and relaxing aroma of our candles.
